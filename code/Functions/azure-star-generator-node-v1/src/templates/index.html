About STAR Method

The STAR method is a structured way to respond to behavioral interview questions:

  • Situation: Set the scene and give context
  • Task: Explain what you were responsible for
  • Action: Describe what steps you took
  • Result: Share the outcomes of your actions

This tool helps you format your work experiences into compelling STAR stories for Azure certification interviews.
